Cadbury Dairy Milk

Cadbury Dairy Milk is one of the most iconic chocolate bars in the world. Since its introduction in 1905, it has been cherished by chocolate lovers of all ages. Made with creamy milk chocolate, each bite of Cadbury Dairy Milk is a moment of pure bliss.

  • Available in various sizes and flavors
  • Perfect for snacking, baking, or gifting
  • Price: Varies based on size and quantity

Cadbury Fruit & Nut

Cadbury Fruit & Nut combines the smoothness of Cadbury milk chocolate with the crunch of almonds and the sweetness of juicy raisins. It's a delightful treat for those who enjoy a blend of flavors and textures in their chocolate.

  • Perfect balance of sweetness and crunch
  • Contains real fruit and nuts for added nutrition
  • Price: Varies based on size and quantity
